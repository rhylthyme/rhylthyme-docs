# Getting Started

!!! info "Screenshots coming soon - placeholder images shown below."

This guide walks you through installing Rhylthyme for iOS and loading your first program.

## Install from the App Store

1. Open the **App Store** on your iPhone or iPad
2. Search for **Rhylthyme**
3. Tap **Get** to download and install

!!! tip
    Rhylthyme requires iOS 17.0 or later. If you don't see the app in search results, check that your device is running a supported version under Settings > General > About.

## First Launch

When you open Rhylthyme for the first time, you land on the **Home** tab. No account is required to browse examples, load programs, or use the visualization.

### Load an Example Program

The fastest way to see Rhylthyme in action:

1. Scroll to the **Examples** section on the Home tab
2. Tap any example -- for instance, **Breakfast Schedule**
3. The app switches to the **Visualization** tab and loads the program

<!-- TODO: Add iPhone screenshot -->
![Examples list on the Home tab](../assets/screenshots/ios/ios-home-examples.png)

The Visualization tab appears in the tab bar only after a program is loaded.

### Start Playback

Once a program is loaded in the Visualization tab:

1. Tap the **Play** button in the native playback controls at the bottom
2. Watch steps execute in real time on the timeline
3. Use the **speed picker** to accelerate playback (1x, 2x, 5x, 10x, 30x, or 60x)
4. Tap **Pause** to freeze execution, or **Stop** to reset

## Optional: Sign In

Signing in is optional but enables saving and sharing programs. Rhylthyme supports three sign-in methods:

- **Google** -- Tap "Continue with Google"
- **Apple** -- Tap "Continue with Apple"
- **Email** -- Enter your email to receive a magic link (no password needed)

See [Account & Settings](account.md) for details.
