## Key Success Factors Across Verticals

### Common Pain Points
1. **Resource constraint management**: Limited equipment, space, or personnel
2. **Timing precision requirements**: Critical windows for quality, safety, or compliance
3. **Human coordination complexity**: Multiple stakeholders with dependencies
4. **Cost of coordination failure**: Quantifiable waste, rework, or lost revenue
5. **Manual process inefficiency**: Time spent on planning and communication

### Technical Requirements Patterns
1. **Integration capabilities**: APIs for existing vertical-specific software
2. **Mobile-first interfaces**: Field workers and frontline staff need mobile access
3. **Real-time communication**: Notifications, alerts, and status updates
4. **Compliance and audit trails**: Regulatory requirements and documentation needs
5. **Scalability and reliability**: Mission-critical operations requiring high uptime

### Go-to-Market Implications
1. **Vertical expertise**: Industry knowledge and terminology important for credibility
2. **Integration partnerships**: Channel relationships with existing software providers
3. **Compliance and security**: Industry-specific requirements for data handling
4. **Custom implementation services**: Many verticals require configuration and training
5. **Reference customers**: Pilot success stories critical for market credibility
